# Sales_dashboard-excel
# Simpson's and Sons — Interactive Sales Dashboard

![Dashboard Preview](dashboard_preview.png)

## Project Overview

Built an interactive Excel dashboard for Simpson's and Sons, 
a nationwide brick-and-mortar retailer selling technology, 
office supplies, and furniture across 50 US states.

The dashboard analyses **9,994 orders from 2011–2014** 
to surface sales trends, profitability insights, and 
regional performance for executive decision-making.

---

## Dashboard Features

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| Charts | 6 dynamic visualizations |
| Slicers | Year, Sub-Category, State |
| Map | US state-level sales map (Bing Maps) |
| Pivot Tables | 6 separate pivot tables |
| Data | 9,994 orders, 50 states, 17 sub-categories |

---

## Six Visualisations

1. **Quarterly Sub-Category Sales Trend** — Multi-line chart showing sales trajectory for all 17 sub-categories from 2011–2014
2. **Yearly Profit & Loss by Category** — Bar chart comparing P&L across sub-categories year over year
3. **US Sales Map** — Geographic heat map showing total sales by state powered by Bing Maps
4. **Sales by Customer Segment** — Horizontal bar chart comparing Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments
5. **Sales by Category** — Horizontal bar showing Technology, Office Supplies, and Furniture performance
6. **Profit Distribution by Category** — Pie chart showing profit contribution percentage per sub-category

---

## Key Insights

- **Technology** led all categories with $860K+ in revenue
- **Tables and Bookcases** showed consistent negative profit — signalling a pricing or cost structure issue
- **Consumer segment** outperformed Corporate and Home Office combined
- **California and New York** were the highest revenue states
- Sales showed consistent **year-over-year growth** from 2011–2014

## How to Use

1. Download `Simpsons_Sons_Sales_Dashboard.xlsx`
2. Open in Microsoft Excel (2016 or later recommended)
3. Navigate to the **DashBoard** tab
4. Use the three slicers to filter:
   - **Sub-Category** — filter by product type
   - **Years (Order Date)** — filter by year (2011–2014)
   - **State** — filter by US state
5. All 6 charts update dynamically with your selections
